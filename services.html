SERVICES

K-12 Assessments

Vega provides custom assessment writing services in Math, Science and English Language disciplines, for test publishing and education companies. The assessments are aligned to academic standards such as The Common Core Standards (USA), NAPLAN (Australia) and CBSE/ICSE (India)….
Assessments for Learning +
  • Formative and Summative Assessments:
    Vega creates original formative and summative assessments aligned to learning objectives. For each learning objective, items are written at different difficulty levels, testing different cognitive strengths. These assessments,along with the rationales that the team provides, help in identifying students’ strengths and weaknesses. They help teachers evaluate how well students have mastered the learning objectives at the end of a fixed time period.
  • Assessments in Courses:
    Vega provides module-level and end-of-course assessments for K12 courses. The team of writers creates assessments using grade-appropriate contexts, similar to the ones in the course modules, to evaluate understanding and mastery of concepts.
  • Practice assessments:
    Vega creates assessments that help students master different topics taught at school. These items are intended to supplement school work. The team has experience in working with different learning management systems to create different types of assessments to make learning more fun.
Benchmark Assessments +
  • Standardised test preparation:
    Vega creates items to prepare students for different K12 standardised tests conducted in many countries. The items help students identify their readiness to take the tests and provide extra practice in problem areas. The items closely mirror the standardised tests in difficulty and style.
  • Math Scholarship Tests
    Vega creates Mathematics Scholarship Tests. These tests contain items with a range of difficulty and are used to identify students of outstanding academic potential.
